Black Myth: Wukong is a game developed by Game Science. The story of the game is adapted from the 16th century Chinese novel The Journey to the West. The game is still under development and is expected to be released by 2023.

In this game, the player plays as a monkeyman who is capable of transforming into any animal or person, surfing on the cloud, and altering the size of his weapon. But according to the first trailer, the monkeyman is not Wukong himself.
