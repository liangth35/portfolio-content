Black Myth: Wukong

Black Myth: Wukong is an upcoming 3D action role-playing game by Chinese developer Game Science. The story is adapted from the 16th century Chinese novel The Journey to the West. A 1986 TV-series on the same story is a shared childhood memory of all Chinese people, which makes the expectation on this game incredibly high. On Aug. 20, 2020, the developer released a 13-minute pre-alpha gameplay video and attracted the attention of gamers around the world. The video got 2 million views on youtube and 10 million views on bilibili in one day. Now, the game is still under development, in to a 2020 interview with IGN China, Game Science said they plan to release Black Myth: Wukong by 2023.

The game is said to be Souls-like, meaning it will feature hardcore fighting and high difficulty.In this game, the player plays as a monkeyman who is capable of transforming into any animal or person, surfing on the cloud, and altering the size of his weapon. These are all Wukong's signature skills, but according to the first trailer, the monkeyman player controls is not Wukong himself. Official information about the plot of the game is very limited, but some players have deduced from the trailers and the name of the game that in the world line of the game, Wukong was chased by heavenly soldiers and failed to become a buddha in the end.

Q: How will Black Myth: Wukong be monetized?
A: The game will be sold as a one-time purchase with DLCs and in-game purchases. However, we promise we will never sell power.

Q: On what platforms will Wukong be launched?
A: PC and other main-stream consoles. Cloud gaming platforms are also under consideration.

Q: How long will the playthrough be? Will there be an online mode?
A: We'd like to keep the answers for ourselves. But our producer prefers solid single-player contents and online coorpration mode.
