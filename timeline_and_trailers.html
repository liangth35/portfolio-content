Development Timeline of Black Myth: Wukong

In 2016, Yang Qi, the art director, proposed the project Black Myth: Wukong. But the team thought it was too early for them to start this project.

In 2017, after the success of Art of War: Red Tides, Game Science started the development of Black Myth: Wukong. The progress was slow due to lack of manpower.

On Aug. 19, 2020, the team released the first trailer in order to recuirt more personnels. Surprisingly, the 13 minute trailer went viral: it got 45 million views on bilibili, a video site in China, and 10 million views on youtube. In a few days, Game Science received more than 10000 resumes from all around the world, while they expected to hire only 100.

In Mar. 2021, Game Science accepted the investment from Tencent Games, but under the condition that Tencent Games doesn't seek control of the company.

On Aug. 19 2021, the second trailer was released. This was the first trailer after the team switched the game engine from UE4 to UE5.

On Aug. 19 2022, the third trailer was released.

The expected release date of the game is no earlier than 2023.
